Chicken

Indulge in a culinary masterpiece with our signature chicken dish at La Poularde. Accompanied by vibrant seasonal vegetables, this dish promises a culinary journey that delights the senses and leaves a lasting impression of exquisite dining.

Beef

Savor the richness of our prime beef dish at The Steakhouse. A tender, juicy steak, expertly seared to a perfect medium-rare, awaits atop a sizzling hot plate. Paired with a velvety red wine reduction sauce, the flavors deepen, offering a symphony of indulgence that dances on the palate.
